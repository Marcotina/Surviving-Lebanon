Time required for fridge to start dropping temperature after power cut:
>>Keep refrigerator and freezer doors closed.
>>If the doors stay closed, food will stay safe for up to: 4 hours in a refrigerator (if temperature remains below 4°C)
>>Certain perishable foods that have been kept at a temperature above 4°C for more than two hours should be thrown out. This includes meat, poultry, fish, soft cheeses, dairy products, eggs, cooked or cut produce, and cooked leftovers.
>>48 hours in a full freezer; 24 hours in a half-full freezer(CDC, 2020)
