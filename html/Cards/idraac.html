IDRAAC, or Institute for Development, Research, Advocacy and Applied Care, was the first NGO in Lebanon dedicated to delivering free mental health services, and awareness, targeting different segments of the population and tailoring the services provided accordingly. They provide services such as therapy, counselling, immediate attention to emergencies, etc.
