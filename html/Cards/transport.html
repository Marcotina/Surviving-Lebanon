Hadeer Bus

Hadeer Bus is an application that provides a safe space for university students and others, by establishing a safe, comfortable, hygienic and systematic public transportation service that you can access through your phone. Hadeer provides a bus with free Wi-Fi, cameras on board, and live bus tracking, to ensure comfort and ease, while also being as cheap or even cheaper than normal Lebanese public transport.

ByBus Transportation

ByBus is a project launched in Jbeil, that allows you to purchase prepaid cards that you can use to pay for the buses that go on 8 lines, with one line going from Jbeil to Beirut. Trips start at 7'000 LBP including the first 5 kilometers, then an additions 700 LBP for each kilometer. You scan the card upon entry and exit, and the amount is deducted.

Loop Scooters

Public scooters that one can rent for a short time by using the app, Loop helps with public transportation, and is a cheap alternative to getting a ride from a bus. If there is a scooter near you, you can simply scan the code and ride the scooter.
