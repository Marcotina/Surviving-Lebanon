Embrace is a Non-profit Organization, that aims to provide mental health resources, and access to care, viewing them as necessary human rights, without any social, personal, or structural barriers impairing one’s ability to access those resources. Embrace offers a variety of services, including a suicide lifeline for people to directly contact trained personnel, to help with emotional distress; a mental health center that tends to beneficiaries’ emotional, medical, and social concerns, a mental health clinic that offers psychotherapy, psychiatry, and group therapy. Embrace tries to lift the stigma of suicide off of Lebanese society by spreading awareness about it, with statistics and studies, to hopefully stop suicide rates’ increase and provide the necessary assistance to the people in need.
